## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette:
```xml
Primary: #FF6B6B (Coral Red)
Primary Dark: #E53935
Accent: #FFA726 (Orange)
White: #FFFFFF
Text Secondary: #757575
Google Red: #DB4437
Background Gradient: FF6B6B → FF8E53 → FFA726
```

### Typography:
- **Headlines**: 32-36sp, Bold
- **Buttons**: 16-18sp, Bold
- **Body**: 14-16sp, Regular
- **Input Text**: 16sp, Dark (#212121)

### Spacing:
- **Large Margins**: 32-48dp
- **Standard Margins**: 24dp
- **Small Margins**: 16dp
- **Button Height**: 60dp (consistent)

### Corner Radius:
- **Inputs**: 16dp
- **Buttons**: 16dp
- **Cards**: 16dp
- **Containers**: Circular (oval)

### Shadows & Elevation:
- **Logo Containers**: 8dp elevation
- **Primary Buttons**: 4dp elevation
- **Text Shadows**: 2dp radius, 40% black

## 💡 Design Principles Applied

1. **Consistency** - Same corner radius, spacing throughout
2. **Hierarchy** - Most important elements largest/boldest
3. **Contrast** - Clear distinction between elements
4. **Balance** - Symmetrical, centered layout
5. **Depth** - Shadows and elevation for 3D effect
6. **Branding** - Food-themed colors and icons
